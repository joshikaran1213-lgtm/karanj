This is a BMI calculator.

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a screening tool that estimates body fat based on height and weight.
Calculated as \(\frac{\text{weight\ in\ kg}}{\text{height\ in\ m}^{2}}\), a BMI between 18.5
and 24.9 is considered a healthy range, while numbers outside this indicate potential health risks.
